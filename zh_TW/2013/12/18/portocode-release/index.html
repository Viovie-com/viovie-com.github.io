Evercode 正式改名為，PortoCode!

December 18, 2013

The functions of PortoCode are the same as Evercode now, but more convenient and intuitive.

There are two main features：

‧Anywhere! Anytime!

As long as you have a mobile device with EVERNOTE app installed, You can check your codes no matter where you are, or what you are doing.

‧Easy to read！

PortoCode can automatically syntax color your codes. After uploading to EVERNOTE, you can read easy-to-read codes on your device.
Try PortoCode!
